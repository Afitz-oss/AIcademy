# Prompt: First-Run Onboarding

> Use this prompt the first time you open Cursor after filling out `LEARNER_PROFILE.md`.
> Copy and paste it into the chat — or just say "I just filled out my profile, let's get started."

---

I just filled out my `LEARNER_PROFILE.md`. Please:

1. Read `LEARNER_PROFILE.md` carefully
2. Read `09_roadmaps/python_first_principles.md` to understand the stage sequence
3. Based on my profile, generate my personalized `08_learner_profile/learning_path.md` with:
   - My goal track and why it fits my stated goals
   - My starting stage and why (not too easy, not too hard)
   - An ordered list of topics I'll work through
   - Estimated timeline based on my time commitment
4. Generate my `08_learner_profile/competency_map.md` with all relevant skills set to `⬜ not started`
5. Write `08_learner_profile/intake_notes.md` with your observations about my profile and initial teaching approach
6. Tell me (in chat) in 3–4 sentences: who you see me as as a learner, where we're starting, and what my first problem will be about

Then give me my first problem.

**Rules:**
- Don't overwhelm me — one concept, one problem to start
- Anchor the first problem in my stated goal (what I want to build someday)
- Follow the problem format in `AGENTS.md`
- Write the problem to `04_practice_log/<today's date>_onboarding/problems.md`
